Integration Sync History Tab

The Integration Sync History tab is available from the Contact and Lead Detail view only if the contact or lead is synchronized with an external integration. The Integration Sync History tab lists each time the record was synchronized between Infor CRM and the external integration.

This view contains the following columns:

  • Date/Time - Date and time the record was last synchronized.
  • Status - Status of the synchronization. Possible statuses include:
    • OK - The synchronization was successful.
    • NotModified - The record was not updated. Either there were no changes to apply or it was determined through conflict resolution that the record should not be updated.
    • InternalServerError - The synchronization encountered an error and was not successful.
    • BadRequest - The synchronization did not occur due to an invalid request or missing parameters.
