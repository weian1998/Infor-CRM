Addresses Tab

The Addresses tab is located in the Account and Contact Detail views. This tab displays all addresses for the selected contact or account. Only the primary address is displayed in the Detail view.

Columns in the tab include: Primary, Primary Billing, Primary Shipping, Description, Attention, Street Address, City, State, Postal Code, and Country.

To view or edit address details

  1. On the Contact or Account Detail view, click Addresses .If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  2. Click Edit.

To add a new address

  1. Click the Add Address button to add a new address.
  2. Follow the steps for adding address information.
