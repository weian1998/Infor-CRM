Deleting Merge Fields

You can remove merge fields from templates from within the E-mail, Letter, or Fax Template Editor.

To delete a merge field

  1. Open the Template Editor.
  2. Click Edit Fields on the toolbar.
    The Infor CRM Mail Merge Field Editor dialog box opens.
  3. From the toolbar, click Remove From Template.
  4. When prompted, click Yes to delete the field.
  5. Click OK to close the Infor CRM Mail Merge Field Editor.
