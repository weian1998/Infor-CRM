Using the Product Detail View

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

Use the Detail view to see, add, or edit information for a selected record. The Detail view consists of information boxes, tabs, and a user-defined middle section.

Products are read-only when accounting integration is enabled. You must manage products in your accounting system and synchronize the changes to Infor CRM.

How Do I?

Open the Product Detail view
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Sales, Support, or Administration, and then click Products to open the Products List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a product, and then click on the product name.

Use the Lookup to search for records

Switch to the List view
  • On the toolbar click the List View button, or click the current group tab.

Use the Task Pane group list or common tasks

View a group

Use common tasks

On the Task Pane, under Common Tasks, you can:

  • Add to Group
  • Remove from Group

Information Boxes

  • Name - The product name.
  • Description - A description of the product.
  • SKU - The product SKU (Storage Keeping Unit).
  • Family - The product family. Click the drop-down arrow and select an item from the list.
  • Status - The status of the product. Click the drop-down arrow and select an item from the list.
  • Cost - The cost of the product.
  • ((Accounting Integration Only)) Unit of Measure - The scale used to measure the quantity of the product. For example, "Each" or "Box of 10".

Additional information may be available if your implementation includes a Back Office Extension. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.
