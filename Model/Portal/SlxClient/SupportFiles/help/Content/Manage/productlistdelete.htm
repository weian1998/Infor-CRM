Deleting a Product from the List

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

You can delete products from the list that is available when adding or editing opportunity or sales order products.

To delete

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Sales, Support, or Administration, and then click Products to open the Products List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a product, and then click on the product name.
  2. On the Products Detail view, click the Delete button.

  3. Click OK.
