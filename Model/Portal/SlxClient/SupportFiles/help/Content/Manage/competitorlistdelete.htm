Deleting a Competitor from the List

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

You can delete competitors from the list that is available when adding or editing opportunity competitors.

To delete

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Competitors to open the Competitors List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a competitor, and then click on the competitor name.
  2. On the Competitor Detail view, click the Delete button.
  3. Click OKto confirm.
