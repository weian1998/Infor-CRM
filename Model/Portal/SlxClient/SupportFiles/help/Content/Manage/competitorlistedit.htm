Editing a Competitor in the List

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

Use the Competitor Detail view to edit competitor information.

To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Administration,, and then click Competitors to open the Competitors List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a competitor, and then click on the competitor's name.
  2. If necessary, change the competitor information.
  3. Click Save.
