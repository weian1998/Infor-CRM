Understanding Multi-Currency

If your company has Infor CRM users (or prospects) in multiple countries using different currencies, your system administrator can enable multi-currency support. If Multi-Currency is enabled, some Infor CRM views will display information or options specific to your selected currency rate. For example, if you are using the British Pound as your default currency, opportunities will display pricing in pounds.

You can set your default currency on the Opportunity tab in options.
