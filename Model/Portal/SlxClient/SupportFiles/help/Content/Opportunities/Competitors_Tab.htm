Opportunity Competitors

Use this view to add, edit, or remove competitors associated with an opportunity and to help you plan your strategy.

How Do I?

Open this view
  • This view appears on the Insert Opportunity view and as a tab on the Opportunity Detail view. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
Add a competitor to an opportunity
  1. Click Add Competitor.
  2. Locate and select the competitor using the lookup.
  3. Click OK.

Edit opportunity competitor information

Remove a competitor from an opportunity

Manage Competitors (not available to all users)

This view contains the following columns:

  • Competitor - Displays the competitor's name.
  • Rating - Displays the competitor's ranking for the current opportunity.
  • Weaknesses - Displays the competitor's areas of weakness.
