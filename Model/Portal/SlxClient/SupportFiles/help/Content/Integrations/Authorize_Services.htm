Authorizing Services

Use the Authorize Services options tab to authorize a connection with an external service. Before you can authorize a service, your administrator must configure the integration and grant access to users. These options only apply when the current user is signed in to the Infor CRM Web Client.
  • This feature may not be available for all users. External integrations must be implemented by your administrator.
  • You must allow pop-ups before you can authorize services. See your browser help for specific instructions on how to allow pop-ups.

To authorize a service

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.
  2. Click the Authorize Services tab.
  3. Click Authorize next to the service you want to authorize.

  4. Follow any prompts to complete the authorization.
