Selecting a Contract

You can use the Select Contract view when the account has more than one service contract and you need to select the appropriate contract to charge the ticket against.

Your security access determines what functions are available. Contact your administrator for any access rights changes. WebViewer users may not access full functionality.

To select a contract

  1. On the Ticket Detail view, click the Contract Find button.
  2. Use the Find Contract view to find a contract, select the contract in the list, and then click OK.

Tickets and ticket activities will be recorded against the contract you selected.
