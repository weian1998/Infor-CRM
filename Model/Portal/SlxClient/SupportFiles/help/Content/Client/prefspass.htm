Changing a Password

You can change your password through the Options in the Infor CRM Web Client.

To change your password

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.
  2. Click the Password tab, type your password in the Current Password box.
  3. In the New Password box, type your new password.
  4. Retype your new password in the Confirm Password box.
  5. .Click Save.
