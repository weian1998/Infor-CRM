Record(s) Not Found

The Record(s) Not Found view appears when you are recording an e-mail message from Outlook to Infor CRM history, and the contact or lead e-mail address is not in the Infor CRM database. The Record(s) Not Found view shows the subject, who the e-mail is from, and to whom the e-mail is being sent.

The e-mail will not be recorded to history in Infor CRM.
