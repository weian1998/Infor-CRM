Pay-From Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Pay-From tab displays associated payment information. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Pay-From tab
  • Open the Bill-To Detail view and click the Pay-From tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
View payment details
  • Click the Name link to go to the Pay-From Detail view.
