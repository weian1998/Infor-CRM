Contact Associations Tab (Back Office Extension)

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Contact Associations tab displays a list of associated contacts. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Contact Associations tab
  • Open the Account, Bill-To, Ship-To, or Pay-From Detail view and click the Contact Associations tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
View account details
  • Click the Name to go to the Contact Detail view.
Send an email
  • Click the Email link for the person you want to email.
