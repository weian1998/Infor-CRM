Using the ERP Persons Detail View

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use the Detail view to see information for a selected record.

How Do I?

Open the Person Detail view.
  • Do one of the following:
    • On the Navigation Bar, click ERP Persons, and then click the person's name.
    • Open the Ship-to, Invoice, Account, or Sales Order Detail view, click the Sales Person tab, and then click the Name link. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
Edit Person information
  1. In the ERP Person Detail view, make your changes.
    Some information may be for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application.
  2. Click Save.

information boxes

The information boxes contain the person information. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.
