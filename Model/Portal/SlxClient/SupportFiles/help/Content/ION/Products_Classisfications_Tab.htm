Product Classifications Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Classifications tab in the Product Detail view displays product classifications from the integrated back office application. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

To view

  1. Open the Product Detail view.
  2. Click the Classifications tab.
