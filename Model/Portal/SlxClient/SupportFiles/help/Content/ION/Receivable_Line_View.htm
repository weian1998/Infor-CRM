Viewing Receivable Line Item Information

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use the Receivable Item Details dialog box to view receivable line information. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

To view

  1. Open the Receivable Detail view and click the Products tab.
  2. Click the Line Number link for the receivable product you want to view.
  3. View the receivable line information.
  4. Click the Close button when finished.
