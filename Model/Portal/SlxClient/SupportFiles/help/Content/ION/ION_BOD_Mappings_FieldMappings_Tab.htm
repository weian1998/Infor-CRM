Field Mappings Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use the Field Mappings tab to view and manage the field mappings. Field mappings are field-level associations that support synchronization between Infor CRM and an ION back office integration.

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

How Do I?

Open the Field Mappings tab
  • On the ION BOD Mapping Detail view, click the Field Mappings tab.
View ION Field Mapping details
  • Click the Name to open the ION Field Mapping Detail view.
Edit field mappings
  1. Select the field mapping you want to edit.
  2. Click in the grid to edit one or more of the following:
    • XPath - The particular section of the BOD that is being processed.In order to edit this field, refer to the Any BOD chapter in the Infor CRM Back Office Extension Installation Guide.
    • Property - The field name of the entity being mapped. Type the Infor CRM property name being mapped.
    • Active - Use this option to turn on or turn off synchronization for this BOD mapping.
  3. Click Save.
Add a field mapping
  1. Click the Insert New Field Mapping button.
  2. Follow the steps for adding a field mapping.
Delete a field mapping

  • Select the record you want to remove and click the Delete button.
