Using the Ship-To Detail View

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use the Detail view to see, add, or edit information for a selected record. The Detail view consists of information boxes, tabs, and a user-defined middle section. 

How Do I?

Open the Ship-to Detail view.
  • Do one of the following:
    • Open the Receivable, Return, or Sales Order Detail view, click the Bill/Ship Address tab, and then click the Ship-to link.
    • Open the Sales Order Detail view, click the Shipment Lines tab, and then click the Ship-to link.
    • Open the Shipment Detail view, click the Ship Address tab, and then click the Ship-to link.
    • Open the Account, or Bill-To Detail view, click the Ship-To tab, and then click the Name link. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.

information boxes

The information boxes contain the primary billing information. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

detail view tabs

Each tab shows specific information you can use for invoice related activities. Click a link to see more information about each tab.

Bill-To Returns
Accounts Contact Associations

Invoices Shipments

user defined section

You can drag and drop tabs into the user defined middle section. The tab will remain there until it is moved back to the lower section.
