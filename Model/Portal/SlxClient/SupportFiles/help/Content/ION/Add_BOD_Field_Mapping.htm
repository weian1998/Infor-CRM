Adding a Field Mapping

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use this view to add a new BOD field mapping. Field mappings match information between Infor CRM and an ION back office integration.

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

To add

  1. Do one of the following:

    • To add a new field mapping, open the ION BOD Mapping Detail view, click the Field Mappings tab, and then click the Insert New Field Mapping button.
    • To clone an existing ION field mapping, open an ION Field Mapping Detail view you want to clone and click the Save and New button.
      Some information is copied from the original record.
  2. Complete the following information:
    BoxDescriptionCan be edited?
