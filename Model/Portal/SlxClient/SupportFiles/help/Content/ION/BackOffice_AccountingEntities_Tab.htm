Accounting Entities Tab (Back Office Extension)

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use the Account Entities tab to view and manage the Back Office Extension accounting systems that synchronize with Infor CRM.

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

How Do I?

Open the Accounting Entities tab
  • On the Back Office Detail view, click the Accounting Entities tab.
View ION Field Mapping details
  • Click the Name to open the ION Field Mapping Detail view.
Edit an account system in the grid
  1. Click in the grid to select the record you want to edit.

  2. Edit one or more of the following:

    • Name - the name of the accounting entity in the integrated application.
    • ID - the identification number of the accounting entity in the integrated application.

  3. Click Save.

Edit an account system

  1. Click the Edit link next to the record you want to edit.
  2. Follow the steps for editing an accounting system.
  3. Click OK.
Add an accounting system
  1. Click the Insert New Field Mapping button.
  2. Follow the steps for adding an accounting system.
Delete a field mapping

  • Select the record you want to remove and click the Delete button.
