Price Lists Tab (Back Office Extension)

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Price Lists tab displays associated pricing information. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

To open the Price Lists tab

  • Open the Back Office Detail view and click the Price Lists tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
