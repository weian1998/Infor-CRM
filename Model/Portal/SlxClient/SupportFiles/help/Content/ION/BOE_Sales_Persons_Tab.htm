Sales Persons Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Sales Persons tab displays a list of associated ERP sales people. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Sales Persons tab
  • Open the Account, Invoice, Ship-To, or Sales Order Detail view and click the Sales Persons tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
View sales person details
  • Click the Name to go to the ERP Person Detail view.
Send an email
  • Click the Email link for the person you want to email.
