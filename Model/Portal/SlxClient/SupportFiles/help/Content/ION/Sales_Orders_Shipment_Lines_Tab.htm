Shipment Lines Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. The Shipment Lines tab in the Sales Orders Detail view displays the shipment information associated with a sales order. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Shipment Lines tab
  • Open the Sales Order Detail view and click the Shipment Lines tab.
View shipment details
  • Click the ERP Shipment ID to go to the Shipment Detail view.
View product information
  • Click the Product link to open the Product Detail view.
View ship-to information
  • Click the Ship-to link to open the Ship-to Detail view.
