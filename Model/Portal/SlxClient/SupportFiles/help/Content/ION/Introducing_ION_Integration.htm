Introducing Infor CRM Back Office Extension (ICBOE)

Infor CRM Back Office Extension (ICBOE) allows you to share information between Infor CRM and a back office system using the ION integration.

Depending on your back office, you may be able to synchronize all or some of the following:

  • Accounts
  • Addresses
  • Contacts
  • Invoices
  • Sales Orders and Quotes
  • Returns
  • Receivables
  • Shipments
  • Bill To information
  • Ship To information
  • Pay From information
