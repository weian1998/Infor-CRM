Shipment Ship Address Tab

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use this tab to view the address associated with a shipment. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Ship Address tab
  • On the Shipment Detail view, click the Ship Address tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
View ship-to details
  • Click the Ship-to link to go to the Ship-to Detail view.
