Receivable Products Tab (Back office)

Infor CRM Back Office Extension only. A Back Office Extension is an ERP system that is configured to send information to display in Infor CRM. Back Office Extension is managed by an administrator. Use this tab to view products associated with a receivable. This information is for display only and must be added or edited in the integrated application. For specific questions please refer to the documentation for the integrated application or contact your administrator.

How Do I?

Open the Products tab
  • Open the Receivable Detail view and click the Products tab.
View shipment line details
  • Click the Line Number to go to the Receivable Item Details view.
View product details
  • Click the Product link to open the Product Detail view.
