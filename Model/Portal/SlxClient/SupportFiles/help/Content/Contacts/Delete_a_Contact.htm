Deleting Contacts

When you delete a contact you also delete the contact's history, notes, and any open activities. You can also delete contacts by deleting the contact's account.

To delete a contact

  1. Find the contact you want to delete.
  2. On the Contact Detail view, click the Delete button.
  3. Click OK to permanently delete the record, or Cancel to cancel deletion of the record.

To delete multiple contacts

  1. From the Contact List view, select one or more records. To select more than one record, hold down [Ctrl] or [Shift], and highlight the records.
  2. On the Task Pane, click Delete.

  3. Click OK.
