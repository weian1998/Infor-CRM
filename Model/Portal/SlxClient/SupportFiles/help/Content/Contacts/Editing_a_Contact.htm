Editing a Contact

Use the Contact Detail view to edit contact information. To undo any changes, click Reset before clicking the Save button.

To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Contacts.
  2. Find the contact you want to edit, and then click the contact name.
  3. On the Contact Detail view, edit the contact information.
  4. Click Save.
