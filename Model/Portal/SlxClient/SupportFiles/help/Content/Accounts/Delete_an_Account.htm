Deleting an Account

When you delete an account, you also delete contact, account, and opportunity history, notes, and any open activities.

To delete an account

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Accounts.
  2. Find the account you want to delete..
  3. On the Account Detail view, click the Delete button.
  4. Click OK.
