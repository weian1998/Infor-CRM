Adding a Custom Module

You can add custom modules to your mobile deployment.

To add

  1. Open the portal in the Portal Editor.
  2. On the Custom Modules tab, click Add custom module.
  3. Browse to your custom module and then select the module-info.json file.
  4. Click Add Module.
  5. Click Save All.
