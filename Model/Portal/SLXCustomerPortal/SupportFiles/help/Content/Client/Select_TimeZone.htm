Selecting a Time Zone

The Infor CRM Customer Portal displays your time zone in the status bar at the bottom of your browser window. The default time zone is based on your local machine's Time Zone setting. If the time zone is incorrect, you can change to another locale within the same time zone.

To change your time zone

  1. Double-click the time zone displayed in the status bar.
  2. In the Time Zone Settings dialog box, click the drop-down arrow and select from the list.

  3. Click OK.
